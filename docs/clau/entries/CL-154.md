ITEM 2 — `mypy --no-project` workaround in worker mypy CI step

Post-PR #23, the worker has a valid `uv.lock` (committed at `apps/team-ingestion-worker/uv.lock`). The step can switch to the orchestrator's pattern:

```yaml

```

(Exact syntax to confirm against the orchestrator step at ci.yml:219-220.)
