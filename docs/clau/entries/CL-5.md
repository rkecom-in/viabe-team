# CL-5 — Meta WhatsApp templates tiered: Tier-A (5 launch-blocking) + Tier-B (17 concierg

22 total Meta WhatsApp templates needed Phase 1 full. For July 15 soft launch with 10 design partners, only 5 templates are launch-blocking: welcome, weekly_approval, opt_out_confirmation, dsr_verification, support_handoff. Tier-A targets: authored May 19, submitted May 21, approved June 30 (allowing 6 weeks for Meta's 30-50% first-submission rejection rate + revision loops). Tier-B (17 templates): refund conversations, breach notifications, DSR completions, monthly reports, trial extensions, ingestion summaries, contacts import confirmation, etc. Fazal handles in concierge mode for design partners until Meta approves. Tier-B authored + submitted by June 15 (parallel with code build); 18+ of 22 approved by August 15 full launch. Concierge load on Fazal decreases as Tier-B approvals trickle in.
