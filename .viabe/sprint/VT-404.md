## Contract (Fazal 2026-06-23 — found in the Sundaram e2e). Launch-relevant.

**Symptom:** after the welcome, onboarding never starts — a 12h stall. **Root cause (CC-diagnosed):**
the onboarding question queue fills LAZILY on the owner's FIRST inbound (WhatsApp free-form needs a
user-opened 24h window), but the `team_welcome` copy says *"You'll receive a WhatsApp message here
when the proposal is ready"* — it tells the owner to WAIT, not reply. A passive owner never opens the
window → onboarding never kicks off. Auto-discovery itself works (rich draft produced).

## Fix — pick one (plan-first; this is template copy → counsel/Meta-aware):
- **(A)** Change `team_welcome` to explicitly invite a reply (e.g. "Reply 'hi' to get started and I'll
  set up your profile"). Re-submit the template to Meta (copy change = re-approval). OR
- **(B)** Proactively send the FIRST onboarding question as an approved **template** (templates send
  outside the 24h window), which both opens the conversation and starts the confirm flow without
  relying on the owner to guess they should reply.
Recommend (B) for reliability (no dependence on owner initiative), with (A)'s nudge as a fallback.

## Acceptance
- After signup, the owner is prompted into the conversation without having to guess; the first
  confirm-question reliably reaches them and onboarding proceeds.
