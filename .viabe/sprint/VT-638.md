## Problem

Autonomy measurement is strong OFFLINE (tm_audit spine, 77-scenario golden pack, blind opus
tier_rescore, x3 variance discipline) but there is NO continuous ONLINE evaluation: once real
traffic flows, nothing samples live transcripts, scores them against the §2 trust-breaker
classes, tracks drift vs the offline golden baseline, or alerts on a Tier-1 class appearing in
production.

## Scope

1. Sampler: per-tenant-safe sampling of live conversation transcripts (PII-redacted, consent-
   respecting — DPDP review on what may leave the DB for judging).
2. Online judge: the SAME blind tier_rescore instrument (§2.1 classes) run on sampled live
   transcripts on a schedule; per-class counters to the ops surface.
3. Drift: compare online class-rates vs the offline golden baseline; alert (Telegram ops
   channel) on any Tier-1 class > 0 in production — the online analog of the trust floor.
4. Wire-but-dormant is acceptable pre-traffic: the BUILD must exist at launch; it activates on
   prod traffic day-1. Connects to Track-C (learning loop / moat).

## Sequencing (Fazal)

Pre-launch, after VT-636 + the trust floor, before the launch gate.
