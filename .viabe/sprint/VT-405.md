## Contract (Fazal 2026-06-23 — found in Sundaram e2e). DESIGN-FIRST.

Fazal: the VTR Ops Console must surface the auto-discovered information, and let the VTR
**drag-drop** a field from a "Discovered" section into the "Confirmed profile" section.

## Cowork design flag (raised once; Fazal decides)
This makes the **VTR** a promotion path for business-profile facts. Today (CL-390) `confirm_draft` is
the **owner-only** promotion gate ("only owner-confirmed fields become fact"), and CL-426 three-way
routing puts identity/authority with the OWNER (VTR resolves knowledge gaps). VTR-assisted confirm
fits the "VTR = onboarding accelerant / decaying human-on-the-loop" model (esp. VTR#1), but it shifts
the accuracy/authority boundary. So design it deliberately:
- **Provenance:** every confirmed field records WHO confirmed it (owner vs VTR) + source (discovery).
- **Identity-level fields** (the CL-426 owner-authority set) stay owner-ratifiable — VTR drag-drop may
  pre-stage them but the owner still confirms identity, or it's clearly marked "VTR-asserted".
- **PII wall (CL-425/426):** business_profile is non-PII so the VTR can see it; do NOT expose any
  customer-PII draft fields in this panel.
- Audit each VTR promotion (like the existing vtr-action audit rows).

## Scope (after the design ruling)
1. Ops Console tenant view: a "Discovered" panel reading `business_profile_draft` (attributes +
   provenance + per-field confidence) alongside the "Confirmed profile" (canonical L1).
2. Drag-drop a discovered field → confirmed (calls a VTR-scoped confirm with provenance=VTR).
3. Reflect confirmation status (discovered / VTR-confirmed / owner-confirmed) per field.

## Gate
Design-first → Cowork review of the authority model BEFORE build (CL-390/CL-426 boundary). Plan-first.
