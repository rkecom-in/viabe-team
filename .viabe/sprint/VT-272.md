# VT-272 — privacy-policy owner_inputs framing (Fazal/counsel-owned)

Follow-through from the VT-269 DPDP ruling (CL-425, Fazal 2026-06-01): owner_inputs
is the sufficient lawful basis for AI sub-processor transmission of customer PII,
**conditioned on** the privacy policy framing it correctly.

**Requirement (Fazal's condition):** the owner_inputs language in the privacy
policy MUST explicitly state that enabling data inputs permits processing by AI
sub-processors, INCLUDING transmission of the owner's customers' data (e.g. ledger
images → Anthropic). This is the framing that makes owner_inputs a sufficient
basis under DPDP without a separate consent flag.
