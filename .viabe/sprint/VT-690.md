## What (Fazal 2026-07-21: day-1 observability of agent decision-making)

Swap the tracing BACKEND from the paid Pydantic Logfire SaaS to Honeycomb EU (free tier), keeping
the `logfire` LIBRARY (free/OSS) for its best-in-class auto-instrumentation. Single chokepoint:
`observability/logfire.py` → `configure_logfire()` now runs `logfire.configure(send_to_logfire=
False, additional_span_processors=[BatchSpanProcessor(Honeycomb OTLP)])`. Enable-switch:
`HONEYCOMB_API_KEY` (was `LOGFIRE_TOKEN`). Captures: DBOS workflow/step spans (global provider
pickup), `instrument_anthropic` (full LLM prompt->response = the agent's thinking I/O),
`instrument_pydantic`. EU endpoint `https://api.eu1.honeycomb.io`; dataset derived from
service.name; content shipped (Fazal ruling), logfire default scrubbing keeps our own secrets out.
