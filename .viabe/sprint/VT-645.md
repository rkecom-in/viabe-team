`_has_clear_send_imperative(token_list, tokens)`: an UNAMBIGUOUS send-imperative (standalone "send"/
"bhejo"/"bhejdo"/… OR the imperative bigram bhej + {do/de/dena/दो/दे/देना}) bypasses the length floor →
approves regardless of length. BOUNDED money-safe: fires ONLY with NO negation, hedge, reject word, or
contrastive qualifier anywhere — any of those keeps the floor's re-confirm. A bare subjunctive/
hypothetical ("agar bhej dein") is NOT imperative (needs a particle). The floor's original purpose
(a rambling msg WITHOUT a clear send-cue → re-confirm) is unchanged. Amends CL-2026-07-12 floor.
