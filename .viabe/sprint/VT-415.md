review the discovered `business_profile` draft + OAuth-connect Google Sheets / WhatsApp, in the WhatsApp
in-app browser). The current gate `requireFazal()` + `FAZAL_TENANT_ID` is a **Phase-1 single-tenant shim**

signed-up owner can't auth as Fazal, and the page loads the hardcoded tenant, not theirs.

  owner-JWT/session from VT-406/408 signup) + **server-derive `tenant_id` from that session** (never
  `FAZAL_TENANT_ID`, never a client field — IDOR).
