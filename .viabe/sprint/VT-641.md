## Problem (measured on deployed dev, ba280a6)

The manager intermittently DROPS the owner's specific request and serves a generic/adjacent answer
instead of routing to the asked action — a Tier-1 breaker (#4 ignored_speech_act, #6 clearly-wrong action).

**Systematic on DEVANAGARI (j08, 3/3 runs):** onboarded tenant, 8 seeded lapsed customers. Owner (Hindi
script) asks for the lapsed list, then "इन 8 ग्राहकों के लिए वापसी ऑफर तैयार कर दो, अभी भेजना मत, पहले दिखाओ"
(prepare a win-back offer for these 8, don't send yet, show me first). Manager:
- Reply 1: "You currently have 8 customers in your ledger." — in ENGLISH, total-framed (owner asked lapsed).
- Reply 2: "Aapke business mein abhi kis cheez par kaam karun—sales badhana, win back, payments, ya
  Shopify/Sheets connect?" — a GENERIC ONBOARDING/CAPABILITY MENU. It IGNORED the win-back request and did
  NOT delegate to sales-recovery. Then re-served the count (loop_stall).
The IDENTICAL journey in Hinglish/Roman (j01) correctly delegates to SR + drafts a campaign — so the
DEVANAGARI SCRIPT flips the routing.

**Intermittent on EN/Hinglish (same class):** j04 run2 — "top customers by spend" answered with "8 lapsed
customers" (wrong question); j09 run1 — "store 2's sales" answered with generic "your profile is set up,
connect data"; j01 — lapsed-count deflected to total in 2/3 runs. 1/3 rate = the router mis-classifies or
low-confidence-falls-back to a generic answer.
