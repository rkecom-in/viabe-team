# VT-568 — stop matching the wrong company

## Live-drill defect (evidence)
`discover_gbp` queries "{business_name} {city}" and takes `items[0]` blind. For RKECOM Services it matched **Reecomps teleservices pvt ltd** (phonetic near-miss, different company), chained its website (reecomps.in), category ("Telecommunications service provider") and about-text into the draft — while GST (which ran later) had the CORRECT legal entity the whole time. Google's own first organic result + AI Overview identified the right company (rkecom.in, AI business intelligence). No cross-check existed.

## Fix
Entity-resolution step between candidate fetch and draft-write: deterministic name-similarity floor + an LLM adjudicator that reasons over the owner's OWN anchors (signup business_name, GST legal/trade name + principal address, owner-provided website if any, organic search results) vs GBP candidates (top N, not [0]). Confident match → accept + record reasoning in provenance. No confident match → FAIL-CLOSED: drop GBP-derived fields entirely (never pollute the draft with a maybe-wrong entity), keep GST facts, anchor website from the owner's own signals/search when defensible. Redrive entrypoint for an existing tenant (drill tenant 861a56a8 gets re-discovered post-deploy).
