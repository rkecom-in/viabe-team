# VT-520 — welcome delivery: UTILITY-category template

## Root cause (from VT-519, now fixed here)
`team_welcome2` was **Meta-approved as `category=MARKETING`** (confirmed via Content API:
`whatsapp_approval.category=MARKETING, status=approved, allow_category_change=true`). Meta declines
delivery of MARKETING-category templates to low-engagement / new recipients — **error 63049**
("Meta chose not to deliver this marketing message"). Send returns `success=True` (accepted); the
**delivery** fails async → the owner never gets the welcome. The VT-404 copy ("welcome to Viabe Team",
"free trial", "To get started", "Reply hi to begin") read as marketing to Meta's classifier.

## The fix
New **UTILITY-category** template `team_welcome3` with strictly-transactional copy (account-set-up
confirmation + continue-your-own-setup — triggered by the owner's own signup, a legitimate utility
message, not marketing). Submitted to Meta as UTILITY so it actually **delivers**.

- **Submitted 2026-07-01 via Twilio Content API** (`POST /v1/Content` + `/ApprovalRequests/whatsapp`,
  `category=UTILITY`). Meta approval is **ASYNC** — `status=received` at submit; delivery works once
  Meta approves. Content SIDs:
  - en `HX3ec52f76cf477cebf80b3eff5835817e`
  - hi `HX1ee2cb5bb504137ff8be5071ee9b7799`
- **Copy (en):** `Hi {{1}}, your Viabe Team account is set up and active until {{2}}. Reply to this message to continue setting up your business profile.`
- **Copy (hi):** `नमस्ते {{1}}, आपका Viabe Team अकाउंट सेट हो गया है और {{2}} तक सक्रिय है। अपनी बिज़नेस प्रोफ़ाइल का सेटअप जारी रखने के लिए इस संदेश का उत्तर दें।` — **CC draft, flag for native review.**
- Variables UNCHANGED: positional `[owner_name, trial_end_date]` = `{{1}},{{2}}`.

## Wiring
- `config/twilio_templates.yaml`: add `team_welcome3` (new SIDs); mark `team_welcome2` DEPRECATED (MARKETING/63049).
- `config/template_routing.yaml`: `welcome.onboarding` + `welcome.trial` → `team_welcome3`.
- `onboarding/signup.py` `_default_welcome`: send `team_welcome3`.
- `.viabe/templates.md`: canonical `team_welcome3` entry; `team_welcome2` marked deprecated.
- Tests repointed to `team_welcome3`: test_output_composer, test_compose_output_tool, test_signup.

## Verify (real, not mock)
- ruff clean; 9 composer routing tests green (welcome → team_welcome3). signup unit test skips locally (no Postgres) — validates on deployed dev.
- **Delivery verification is deferred to Meta approval:** once Meta approves `team_welcome3` (async), Fazal's next signup re-run delivers the welcome (delivery CALLBACK = `delivered`, not just accepted). **CC does NOT send to Fazal's number — he drives the re-run.**

## Follow-up (NOT in this row — needs its own VT)
**Delivery-status callback (B1 / VT-519 async-delivery follow-up):** there is NO outbound
`status_callback` anywhere in the codebase today — the welcome terminal cannot reflect
`owner_notification_status` (pending → delivered/failed) because Twilio's async delivery result
(incl. 63049) is never captured. That is net-new infra (status-callback endpoint + persistence +
wiring `status_callback` into `messages.create`) = a separate coherent row. Recommend rostering it;
without it, a MARKETING-reclassification of team_welcome3 (Meta `allow_category_change=true`) would
again fail silently.
