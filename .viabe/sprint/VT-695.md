## What

The GST identity confirm goes out as a STRUCTURED card instead of the one-line semicolon blob:
bold business name, constitution line, 📍 address / 💼 nature / 🧾 GSTIN-tail field rows, then
"Is this your business?" with the same Yes/No/Skip quick-reply buttons.

## How

- **New Content object `journey_gst_card`** (canary `canaries/vt695_gst_card_create.py`):
  en `HXdf097aab878f962d3621b619f3f1c0f0` · hi `HXfcf1872a751b277a7c8a49e6a89a91d9`.
  WhatsApp formatting lives in the STATIC body (Meta forbids newlines in variables); the 5
  variables are single-line fields: {{1}} name · {{2}} constitution · {{3}} address ·
  {{4}} nature · {{5}} GSTIN tail. Registered in `config/twilio_templates.yaml` +
  `.viabe/templates.md`.
- **`gst_identity_card_question` now carries `card_vars`** (whatsapp_journey.py) — per-field
  single-line values; blob prompts stay as the fallback. `_clean_addr` title-cases the ALLCAPS
  GST address and drops duplicate segments ("MUMBAI, Mumbai" → "Mumbai"); `_gst_display` adds
  `constitution`.
- **All THREE card send paths route through the new object** (`journey._send_gst_card`):
  1. walker `_send` confirm branch (gst_identity + card_vars → card object first);
  2. turn-brain card-priority injection → `_send_turn(card_vars=…)`;
  3. the proactive discovery push (`push_next_question_after_discovery` payload
     `interactive_template=journey_gst_card` + `interactive_variables`; drainer honors
     multi-variable objects).
  Fallback ladder unchanged: card → blob confirm object → freeform text.
