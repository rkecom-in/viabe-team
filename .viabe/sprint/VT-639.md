## Problem

On the onboarding off-taxonomy path, when the owner answers the business-type prompt with a
pure GST "nature of business" tax code (e.g. "Supplier of Services") — a deflection, not a
description — the deterministic journey walker (`journey.py`) stored it raw as the confirmed
`business_type` (line ~546 `value = body.strip()`). The VT-601 cross-fill then copied that same
raw sentence into `about`, and `_completion_recap` echoed the identical value twice
("Here's what I've noted: X, X"). Two defects:

1. **GST-nature deflection recorded as the business identity** — taxonomy-validity alone can't
   tell a GST deflection from a legit rich description (both are non-taxonomy; VT-601 salvages
   the latter intentionally), so the walker had no guard.
2. **Doubled recap** — the VT-601 cross-fill produces `business_type == about`, and the recap
   emitted both.

## Fix

`apps/team-orchestrator/src/orchestrator/onboarding/journey.py`:

- `_GST_NATURE_DEFLECTION_PHRASES` — narrow tuple of pure tax-activity phrases (supplier of
  services/goods, recipient of goods or services, works contract, input service distributor,
  office/sale office, warehouse/depot, bonded warehouse, leasing business, service provision).
  Retail/wholesale/manufacturer EXCLUDED — those are usable sectors.
- `_is_gst_nature_deflection(body)` — case-insensitive substring match.
- `_reprompt_gst_nature(q)` — returns re_present=True with "That's your GST tax category, not
  what your business does — what do you actually make or sell?" (en+hi). Does NOT echo/reject
  the draft (it's a deflection, not a "no").
- `handle_reply` confirm branch: `if field == "business_type" and not (toks & _YES) and
  _is_gst_nature_deflection(body): return _reprompt_gst_nature(q)` — re-asks, does NOT record.
- `_completion_recap`: `seen: set[str]` case-insensitive dedup — identical values collapse to
  one, killing the "noted: X, X" doubling.

Commits: `bac84ee` (walker deflection re-ask) + `28b1502` (recap dedup follow-on). On origin/dev.
