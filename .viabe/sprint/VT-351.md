# VT-351 — kg_events outbox at-rest payload retention (privacy-posture decision)

Flagged by the VT-327 plan-ack (Cowork 20260606T070000Z). VT-327 closed the DSR coverage
(kg_events is hard-deleted on a tenant DSR). This is the SEPARATE, non-DSR question:

The KG outbox drain only stamps `drained_at` — it never deletes or redacts the row. So for an
ACTIVE (non-DSR) tenant, the raw `business_name` (TENANT_CREATED payload) sits in
`kg_events.payload` indefinitely post-drain. For a business's OWN identity in its OWN
RLS-scoped outbox this is likely acceptable retention (not a CL-390 violation) — but it is
exactly the "outbox payload retains raw PII at rest; check purge-on-drain, not just the
downstream store" pattern, worth a tracked decision.

**Decision for Fazal (NOT a build — roster only):** redact/null the PII payload fields on
drain (the downstream l1_entities projection already holds what the KG needs), vs accept
own-tenant outbox retention. If "redact on drain" → a small change to the drain path; if
"accept" → close with a documented rationale.
