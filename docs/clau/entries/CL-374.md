# CL-374 — DECISION: Three compliance items CLOSED — Anthropic DPA (done), Twilio/WhatsApp 

Three compliance items are formally CLOSED. No session may treat any of them as an owner_inputs blocker.

=== 1. ANTHROPIC DPA — CLOSED, no action ===
Anthropic's Data Processing Addendum (including Standard Contractual Clauses) is automatically incorporated into the Commercial Terms of Service. Accepting the Commercial Terms = accepting the DPA. Viabe uses the Anthropic API directly under Commercial Terms, so the DPA is in force with no separate signing step. Confirmed 2026-05-23 from Anthropic's own published guidance. Status: DONE. Optional only: save a PDF of the DPA for the compliance file.

=== 2. TWILIO / WHATSAPP TERMS — CLOSED, verified, no blocker ===
Verified 2026-05-23:
(a) Twilio's general Acceptable Use Policy does NOT restrict forwarding inbound WhatsApp message content to a third-party LLM. Conduct-based policy only. Two clauses place the compliance burden on Viabe by name: 'Violations of Laws or Standards' (obtain consent where law requires) and 'Data Safeguards' (customer responsible for determining adequate safeguards).
(b) Twilio's Predictive & Generative AI/ML Features Addendum does NOT apply to Viabe — it governs Twilio's own native AI features. Viabe calls the Anthropic API directly, outside that addendum.
(c) WhatsApp Business Solution Terms permit using an LLM API (Anthropic) to process business/customer-service interactions, subject to: business data (incl. derived/anonymized) must not train external AI models; the provider must act strictly as a data processor; DPDP requires explicit consent before processing user data with an external LLM. Conditions (i) and (ii) are satisfied by Anthropic's Commercial Terms (no training on Customer Content). Condition (iii) is the DPDP consent + privacy-notice obligation, owned by Fazal, handled in the privacy-notice workstream.
Status: DONE. No architectural risk. Optional residual: a written Twilio support confirmation for the compliance file — not required to proceed.

=== 3. ZERO DATA RETENTION (ZDR) — CLOSED as DEFERRED ===
Decision (Fazal, Type 3, 2026-05-23): ZDR is NOT pursued for launch. The Anthropic default — 30-day backend deletion, no training on Customer Content — is the launch data-handling posture and is sufficient for a defensible, DPDP-compatible privacy notice. Rationale: (a) the 30-day default already supports a sound, signable privacy notice; (b) ZDR requires an approval process that adds friction on the critical path to the July 15 soft launch; (c) the 30-day window retains useful abuse-screening and debugging history while the product is new. ZDR is revisited post-launch as an enhancement when Viabe has scale and/or a design partner that specifically requires zero retention. Status: DEFERRED — not an open item, not a blocker.

=== NET ===
The owner_inputs MERGE gate that previously read 'Anthropic DPA in force' is satisfied. No compliance item blocks the owner_inputs build or merge. The only remaining Fazal-owned compliance obligation is the privacy-notice wording (DPDP consent + disclosure), which is drafted after the body-retention fix lands and is tracked in the closeout tracker, not here.
