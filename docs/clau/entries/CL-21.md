# CL-21 — Step record retention: 90 days full, then aggregated to Pipeline_Run_Summary

Retention: 90 days of full step_records with typed envelopes. After 90 days, records are aggregated to a `Pipeline_Run_Summary` (run_id, tenant_id, start_time, end_time, status, final_outcome, total_cost, step_count, error_summary if any) and full step_records are dropped. Privacy audit log (VT-80) keeps its hash-chained record separately with its own retention (DPDPA-aligned, 7-year minimum). Aggregation runs nightly via scheduled trigger. Cost model: at 100 tenants × 5 weekly campaigns × 30 steps/run × ~10KB/step = ~15MB/week post-launch; 90-day window holds ~190MB; aggregated to ~5KB/run thereafter. Acceptable cost trade-off per Fazal's 'minimal possible cost' constraint.
