## What CoderC must produce
The Method 5 ingestion adapter. Owner sends a cash-book photo + a voice note narrating the entries ("this page is from yesterday, Rajesh paid 500, Sunita paid 300…"). Adapter combines vision extraction + voice transcription + LLM merge of both signals.
1. **Adapter module** at `apps/team/src/ingestion/methods/cash_book.py`:
	- `ingest_cash_book(tenant_id, image_url, audio_url, run_id) -> IngestionSummary`.
	- `audio_url` is optional. Image-only ingestion works (degrades to VT-6.4 paper-book pattern). Audio-only ingestion works (degrades to voice-only entries).
2. **Voice transcription** at `apps/team/src/ingestion/voice_transcription.py`:
	- Fetches audio from Twilio (handles `audio/ogg`, `audio/m4a`, `audio/mp3`).
	- Calls Anthropic's audio model (or OpenAI Whisper as Phase 1 fallback if Anthropic doesn't support audio at Phase 1; documented decision in `models.yaml`).
	- Returns `TranscriptionResult`: `transcript_text`, `language` (en/hi/mixed), `confidence`, `tokens_used`, `cost_paise`.
3. **Multimodal merge**:
	- Vision extraction from image → list of structured entries (per VT-6.1 PaperBookSchema).
	- Voice transcription → free-text narration.
	- LLM merge call (Sonnet 4.6, separate from vision call) given both inputs: "Here's the structured extraction from the cash book photo. Here's the owner's narration. Reconcile: which entries from the photo does the owner reference? Are there entries the owner mentioned that aren't in the photo? Are there entries in the photo the owner didn't mention?"
	- Returns merged candidate entries with reconciliation notes.
4. **Confidence**:
	- Entries confirmed by both photo + audio: confidence boosted (0.95).
	- Entries from photo only or audio only: confidence stays at the lower of the two source confidences.
	- Conflicting entries (photo says ₹500, audio says ₹700): low confidence (0.5) → clarifying flow.
5. **Confidence routing**: same thresholds as VT-6.1.
6. **Tests** at `apps/team/tests/ingestion/methods/test_cash_book.py`:
	- Photo-only path: image with 5 entries → 5 candidates extracted.
	- Audio-only path: voice note narrating 5 entries → 5 candidates extracted.
	- Multimodal happy path: photo + audio agreeing on 5 entries → 5 candidates with boosted confidence.
	- Conflict: photo and audio disagree on 1 amount → 1 entry routed to clarifying flow.
	- Audio fetch failure: fall back to photo-only path.
	- Photo fetch failure: fall back to audio-only path.
	- Both fail: structured error.
	- Cross-tenant: tenant_A inputs cannot create tenant_B rows.
	- PII absence in logs.
	- Hindi audio transcription: synthetic Hindi voice note → correct transliteration / extraction.
	- Real-API integration test (env-gated): real fixtures.

## Architectural rules to enforce in this subtask
- Pillar 4: invented entries forbidden. If audio mentions "Rajesh 500" but photo doesn't show Rajesh, the entry is captured with audio-only-confidence and routed appropriately — NOT silently merged with hallucinated photo support.
- Pillar 8: vision + audio + merge are separate primitives. Cash-book adapter coordinates; doesn't reimplement.
- Audio storage policy identical to image: NEVER persisted; in-memory only.
- Voice transcription model documented in `models.yaml` under `voice_transcription`. Phase 1 is OpenAI Whisper if Anthropic audio not yet supported; switch is config when supported.
- Conflicting entries always route to clarifying flow. Auto-resolution forbidden.

## Acceptance criteria
- Adapter handles photo-only, audio-only, and multimodal paths.
- Voice transcription module exists with Whisper / Anthropic-audio backend.
- LLM merge call produces reconciled candidates with confidence values per merge type.
- Conflict path triggers VT-6.2 clarifying flow.
- All fetch-failure fallback paths tested.
- Hindi audio transcription tested.
- Cross-tenant attack passes.
- PII absence in logs.
- Audio bytes never persisted.
- Real-API integration test passes (env-gated).
- mypy --strict passes.
- PR title references `(VT-IntegrationInvention)`; reviewed by CoderX.

## Out of scope
- Long-form audio (Phase 1 caps at 2-minute voice notes; longer rejected with structured error).
- Multi-page cash book in single upload (Phase 1: one page = one upload).
- Audio-only narration without prior photo (works but degraded; no concept of "cumulative voice ledger" — each upload is an event).
- Tamil/Telugu voice transcription (Phase 2; Phase 1 is Hindi + English).
- Audio archival for owner replay (out of scope).
