# VT-277 — Capture tenant primary VPA + filter non-tenant UPI VPAs

Follow-up split out of VT-57 (Cowork D3 ruling, 2026-06-01). The VT-57 row wanted
"drop UPI transactions to VPAs other than the tenant's primary (captured at
sign-up)". No `tenant.primary_vpa` substrate exists, so VT-57 SKIPPED that filter
for Phase-1 (the export is the owner's own statement — single-UPI SMBs are the
common case).

This row adds it:
1. Capture the tenant's primary UPI VPA(s) at onboarding (ties to VT-267 owner UX).
2. In upi_export, drop credits received on a NON-tenant VPA (some owners have a
   personal + a business UPI; only the business one is customer activity).

Deferred-not-dropped: until then a multi-UPI owner's personal credits would import
as customer activity — acceptable Phase-1 risk per Cowork (most SMBs run one UPI).
