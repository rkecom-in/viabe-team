# CL-342 — DECISION: Row A resolved — owner_inputs LLM-transmission is permitted under both

## What Row A asked

Whether transmitting inbound WhatsApp owner-message content to a third-party LLM (Anthropic) for extraction is permitted — a gate that, if it failed, would have killed the owner_inputs extraction design.

## Verified finding — Meta side

WhatsApp Business Solution Terms (Restrictions clause) PERMIT sharing Business Solution Data with a third party, via an explicit carve-out: '...except to a Third Party Service Provider in accordance with these WhatsApp Business Solution Terms.' Condition: the Third Party Service Provider must agree IN WRITING to use the data only for the customer's purposes, consistent with WhatsApp's terms. The customer remains fully liable for the TPSP's acts. This is not a prohibition — it is a permitted, conditioned path.

## Verified finding — Anthropic side

Anthropic Commercial Terms of Service (Section B, Customer Content): 'Customer retains all rights to its Inputs and owns its Outputs... Anthropic may not train models on Customer Content from Services.' Anthropic data-handling docs confirm: no training on API data by default; 30-day backend deletion for API customers (90 days only for AUP-flagged content); Zero Data Retention agreement available for qualifying commercial customers; a Data Processing Addendum is offered. The September 2025 consumer-account changes (5-year retention, train-by-default) explicitly EXCLUDE Commercial Terms / API usage — they do not apply to Viabe.

## Conclusion

The chain holds end to end: Meta permits onward transfer to a TPSP; Anthropic's Commercial Terms + DPA satisfy the written-agreement condition. owner_inputs is NOT at architectural risk. Row A is not a kill-gate.

## Row A reclassified

From 'Critical gate that may kill the approach' to 'confirmation + DPA execution + privacy-notice drafting.' Three concrete actions remain, none of which gate the owner_inputs DESIGN or BUILD — they gate the MERGE only:
1. Confirm Viabe's Anthropic account is on Commercial Terms (API default), not a consumer plan.
2. Execute Anthropic's Data Processing Addendum; request Zero Data Retention. The DPA is the written processor agreement that satisfies both the WhatsApp TPSP condition and DPDP's processor requirement.
3. Privacy-policy disclosure (Fazal-owned, mandatory regardless): the owner-facing privacy policy must state plainly that message content is transmitted to third-party AI processors (Anthropic) for processing, that those processors are contractually barred from training on it, and that it is deleted within 30 days (or not retained, under ZDR).

## Consequence for sequencing

owner_inputs (Row D) may be briefed and BUILT in parallel with Fazal executing the Anthropic DPA. The build does not depend on the DPA; only the MERGE does. Row A no longer blocks build-facing work.

## Open verification gap (honest note)

The Twilio layer was not independently verified. Viabe reaches WhatsApp via Twilio; Twilio is a standard WhatsApp BSP and the architecture is conventional, so risk is low — but Twilio's WhatsApp policy should be read to confirm no added constraint. This is the one remaining read to make Row A fully airtight.
