Anthropic's base models; (b) Anthropic must act strictly as a data processor for Viabe's exclusive operational needs; (c) DPDP — explicit, unambiguous consent before processing user data with an external LLM. All three are satisfied: Anthropic's Commercial Terms explicitly bar training on Customer Content (covers a+b); (c) is the privacy-notice/consent obligation tracked below.
