# CL-390 — DECISION (Fazal, LOCKED/STANDING): (1) Third-party exchanges Anthropic + Twilio 

Fazal locked a set of privacy/data-exchange decisions. These are STANDING and must not be re-litigated by future sessions.

=== LOCKED DECISIONS ===

1. THIRD-PARTY DATA EXCHANGES ARE MANDATORY, NOT OPTIONAL, NOT SWITCHABLE. The system cannot function without them. They are baked into the privacy policy as required processing, consent-gated as the cost of using Viabe. The locked exchanges:
   - ANTHROPIC: customer message content sent for inference (Sales Recovery Agent reasoning + Composer reply drafting; and Haiku owner-input classification once owner_inputs is ON). Inference only; Anthropic contractually barred from training on it.
   - TWILIO: WhatsApp message transport.
   - VOYAGE: text sent to produce embeddings for the L1 Knowledge Graph. DECISION: the Voyage embedding path WILL receive RAW customer message content (not only derived/internal text). This is accepted and factored into the policy.
   - DBOS durability layer: transient ~2.5h retention of raw body for crash-recovery, then auto-purge (PR #47).

2. owner_inputs FEATURE: ON AT JULY LAUNCH. Fazal's product decision. owner_inputs lets the business owner steer/override the agent's understanding of a topic or customer via plain-language instructions. The OWNER_INPUTS_EXTRACTION_ENABLED flag is an ENGINEERING feature-flag (ship-safety catch), NOT a privacy/consent control — it is currently OFF only because the feature is not yet verified-live. STRING ATTACHED: the feature must be properly verified before the flag flips ON for July; 'ON for launch' is conditional on that verification.

3. PRIVACY NOTICE IS SYSTEM-LEVEL (CL-384): covers all of Viabe's customer-data handling, not the owner_inputs feature alone.

=== HOW FUTURE SESSIONS MUST TREAT THIS (anti-blocker instruction) ===

DO: treat the four exchanges above (Anthropic, Twilio, Voyage incl. raw bodies, DBOS hold) as SETTLED. Build on them freely. Do NOT re-investigate, re-question, or pause development to re-confirm whether these exchanges are 'allowed' — they are locked.

DO NOT over-read this as 'privacy is done, never stop for it'. The agreed policy is: these exchanges are permitted PROVIDED the privacy notice discloses them AND consent is obtained. That proviso is the deal. Therefore still-real, still-gating work: (a) drafting + lawyer-review + publishing the privacy notice; (b) building the consent-capture mechanism in the owner-facing flow; (c) any GENUINELY NEW data flow not in the locked list above must still be surfaced. The unblock applies to the SETTLED LIST, not to privacy as a category.

=== CONSEQUENCE TO TRACK ===
Raw customer message content going to Voyage means L1 KG vector rows are customer subject-data. The DSR-purge surface must include L1 KG vector rows (l1_entities/l1_relationships embeddings) for deletion-request completeness. Verify dsr_purge covers these (it lists owner_inputs at dsr_purge.py:113; confirm L1 KG tables are also in _PURGE_ORDER).
