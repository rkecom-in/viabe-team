## Defect (live-measured)

Turn-brain said "Last one - when's demand typically highest…", owner answered, and the walker
then asked "What are your typical operating hours?" — the trust-killer. Root cause: the ≤3
residual cap (`_MAX_GAPS_WITH_DRAFT`) applied per-RECOMPOSE — every answered question freed a
slot for a fresh one, so the total was unbounded.

## Fix

1. **Honest TOTAL budget** (`question_brain.compose_onboarding_questions`): every residual the
   owner has ALREADY answered debits the budget (identity captures business_name/owner_name/
   gst_identity and confirm-style answers — fields the draft itself carries — never debit).
   3 residual answers + a draft → ZERO further gaps, whatever the LLM proposes. Budget checked
   BEFORE append; the gap LLM is not even called when the budget is spent (hot-path save).
2. **Web-presence FIRST** (Fazal: the single highest-value capture): a deterministic gap
   question — "Does your business have a website or an online page — Google, Facebook,
   Instagram, LinkedIn or IndiaMART? Paste the link…" (suggestion: "No website") — leads every
   fresh gap set, consumes budget like any residual, is suppressed when the draft already has
   a website or the owner answered/skipped it. `_FIELD_ALIASES` collapses every website/social
   synonym the LLM invents onto `web_presence`; `covered_by_draft` covers it via draft.website.
3. **Answer promotion** (`whatsapp_journey.promote_answers_to_tenant`):
   `extract_web_presence_url` (alpha-TLD regex — "8.30 to 9" never reads as a domain;
   https-normalized) → `write_draft {website}` source=owner. Refusals/free text never land.
4. **Turn-brain honesty line** (belt): never say "last"/"one more"/"finally" unless the
   still-needed list has EXACTLY ONE item.
