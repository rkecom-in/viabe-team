# VT-291 — Ops Console — Fleet

## Why
The VTR needs at-a-glance health of the owner-agent fleet across their assigned
businesses to spot trouble before it escalates.

## What
- Listing: agent fleet health/status per business, inline filters (status, business),
  inline actions.
- Drill into a business/agent → right-drawer overlay (per the shell's overlay primitive).

## Acceptance criteria
- VTR sees only assigned-tenant agents (RLS fail-closed; real-PG canary).
- Inline status accurate; drill-overlay opens with real data + actions (no dead-ends).
- PII de-identified (CL-426).

## Out of scope
- The live activity stream itself (VT-293); escalation handling (VT-292).
