# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) — Viabe Team

**DRAFT — NOT legally validated. Pending counsel review (Fazal).** Drafted by Cowork; not legal advice.

Applies to every Business and user of Viabe Team. Breach may lead to suspension or termination. **Version:** v0-draft.

## Prohibited use
You must not use Viabe Team to:
- Send **unsolicited / spam** messages, or message customers who have **not opted in** or who have opted out.
- Violate **WhatsApp/Meta Business & Commerce policies** (incl. prohibited goods, prohibited content, opt-in/template rules) or **TRAI/DLT** rules for SMS.
- Send unlawful, deceptive, harassing, hateful, or infringing content; impersonate another person or business.
- Distribute malware, attempt unauthorised access, scrape beyond permitted use, or circumvent platform protections.
- Process personal data without a lawful basis, or use the service to harm Data Principals.

## WhatsApp-specific discipline (protects sender standing)
- Message only opted-in customers; honour STOP/opt-out immediately.
- Use approved templates for business-initiated messages; respect the 24-hour window rules.
- Do not artificially inflate contact lists or send high-velocity low-engagement blasts (triggers Meta enforcement).

## Enforcement
We may investigate suspected violations and suspend/limit the account to protect the platform, other tenants, and our WhatsApp sender standing. Serious or repeated violations → termination.
