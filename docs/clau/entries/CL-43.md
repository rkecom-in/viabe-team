# CL-43 — DLT for Vodafone Idea is VILPOWER, not Smartping

Correcting prior references in execution plan and resurrection file: DLT registration with Vodafone Idea is done via the VILPOWER portal at https://www.vilpower.in/, NOT 'Vodafone Idea Smartping'. Smartping (smartping.live) is the Videocon DLT portal, a different operator entirely. Source: Plivo + Authkey + Vilpower official docs, verified 2026-05-15.

The six TRAI-approved DLT operator portals are:
- Vodafone Idea (Vi): vilpower.in (Vilpower)
- Jio: trueconnect.jio.com
- Airtel: dltconnect.airtel.in
- BSNL: ucc-bsnl.co.in
- Tata Tele: telemarketer.tatateleservices.com
- Videocon: smartping.live (Smartping)

Key facts for our registration:
- DLT registration on ANY one portal propagates Entity ID + headers + templates across all operators (shared ledger). We only register on ONE.
- Vilpower fee: ₹5,900/year (inclusive of GST).
- Entity registration approval: 3-7 business days.
- Header (sender ID) approval: 1-5 business days after entity.
- Template approval: 24-48 hours after header.
- Documents: Business PAN, GST/Incorporation/Shop&Establishment cert, LOA on letterhead, Authorized Signatory Letter, business email.

Fazal is registering on Vilpower today (May 15) per Day 1 execution plan.
