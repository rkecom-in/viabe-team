# VT-290 — Ops Console shell + Home/Triage

## Why
Foundational shell for the VTR/VTAdmin control panel (VT-189). One console, two roles
(VTR = assigned businesses; VTAdmin = all + assignment control). Home is urgency-first:
the VTR lands on what needs attention now.

## What
- Single-shell app (one nav, all ops pages inside it). Role gate: VTR vs VTAdmin.
- Home/Triage: KPI tiles, escalation queue, "needs review" items, immediate-actions,
  urgency notifications. Every tile/number resolves to a real action or a real view
  (no dead-ends).
- Listing + inline filters + actions pattern (NO separate detail pages — deep views are
  overlays). Establish the right-drawer overlay primitive other pages reuse.

## Acceptance criteria
- VTR sees only assigned-tenant data; VTAdmin sees all (RLS fail-closed, CL-82/88; real-PG canary).
- PII de-identified from the VTR view (CL-426).
- Every Home tile/number links to a real action/view (no placeholder).
- Overlay primitive works (open over dimmed listing, own actions, close).

## Out of scope
- The individual pages (Fleet/Escalations/etc. — VT-291..296) + Telegram (VT-297/298).
