## Contract (rostered 2026-06-13 — Fazal: "go with Option b")

Close the concept↔build gap surfaced 2026-06-13: Viabe Team is positioned for "both
physical and digital businesses," but the live build is physical/local-SMB-first. A
purely digital business (D2C/Shopify store, online-only seller, creator, services) cannot
be cleanly onboarded today.

**Current state (verified on dev `0874370`):**
- `config/business_types.yaml` is 17 physical local categories (kirana, restaurant, salon,
  pharmacy, …) + "Other." No online-store / D2C / creator / digital-services option.
- Auto-discovery (`onboarding/auto_discovery.py`) is GBP-first (`apify_gbp`) — a
  physical-location product; a no-storefront business gets little. Website extraction works
  but is secondary; `discover_serper` is still a stub.
- Digital SEEDS already exist and should be reused, not rebuilt: the **Shopify connector**
  (`integrations/connectors/shopify.py`), **sale-based** recovery
  (`sales_recovery_executor` keys on `days_since_last_sale`, channel-agnostic), UPI export,
  and the Integration Agent connector framework.

**Scope (two parts):**
1. **Taxonomy** — extend `business_types.yaml` with digital categories (e.g. online store /
   D2C, digital services / freelancer, creator/content, services-no-storefront), EN+HI
   labels, via the existing single-source dropdown (VT-96). KG vocab + any business_type
   consumers updated.
2. **Online-store-first discovery** — a discovery path that, for a digital tenant, leads
   with website + (optionally) a Shopify/online-store connect instead of GBP, and degrades
   honestly when there's no physical listing. Reuse the Shopify connector; do NOT auto-import
   without the owner's connect/consent (CL-421 zero-manual-paste-after-OAuth still applies).

**Acceptance:**
- A digital-only test tenant can sign up with a matching business type and reach a grounded
  plan without a GBP listing (no empty/garbage profile).
- Canary on the discovery path (Rule #15) — real connector/website call, fail-not-skip.
- Thin-input honesty preserved (no fabricated facts for a sparse digital profile).
