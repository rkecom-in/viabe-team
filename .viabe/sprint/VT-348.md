# VT-348 — team_edge_case_ack must be Meta-approvable (all-variable body rejected)

Found by Cowork (20260606T030000Z) while preparing the Meta-template whitelist spec.

`team_edge_case_ack` (VT-84) has an effectively all-`{{1}}` body (a generic carrier holding the
handler's full reply text). **Meta rejects template bodies that are entirely/almost-entirely a
variable** (a WhatsApp template needs meaningful static content + Meta caps variable density).
So as-is it can NEVER get a SID → the exclusion / status_query edge-case handler replies stay
**fail-closed (no owner ack)** even after Fazal provisions everything else. **Launch-relevant:
the VT-84 edge-case acks can't go live without this**, regardless of SID provisioning.

## Options (Fazal's call — behind his template-provisioning)
- **(a) static wrapper** — body = "Update from your Viabe agent: {{1}}" (one Utility template,
  simple; Cowork leans (a) for launch).
- **(b) per-intent fixed templates** — exclusion-confirmed / status-answer / … each with real
  static copy (cleaner, more Meta-friendly, more templates).

When Fazal picks, adjust the edge_case handler send-path (`edge_cases_router._send_edge_ack`)
to the chosen shape + register the new template(s). DON'T build before the a/b ruling.

Cross-ref: the NEEDS-FAZAL template-SID gate on VT-89 (this is a sub-blocker of it — the SID
can't even be requested until the body shape is fixed).
